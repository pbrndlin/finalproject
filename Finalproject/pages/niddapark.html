The niddapark:

close by regeneration area

 

One can get exhausted very easily of the pulsating but noisy city of frankfurt.

Ginnheim has great opportunities to relax, take a walk in the nature or go biking along the river of Nidda. Only five minutes away from oldtown Ginnheim there is the Niddapark, one of the biggest parks around the city.

It is my favorite place so far because it is so close to the city, but within minutes you feel like you are in the middle of nowhere. Please enjoy the short video to get an impression of how beautiful it is.
