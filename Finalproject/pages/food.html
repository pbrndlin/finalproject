The perfect mix of traditional Frankfurt food and international Influence 

 

The frankfurt cuisine is very unique for some meals. I already showed you Frankfurter Green Sauce in a previous project, which is probably the most unique one. Another very typical meal is Handkäs, a special cheese - Both meals  usually come with a Apfelwein (apple wine). Around Ginnheim you can find a lot of places that serve those meals. Especially close to the river there are various typical frankfurt restaurants.

 

Just a small advise for a tourists: you should start with the green sauce, which is less unique.

Besides this, Ginnheim also has some international food. One that you can find all over Germany and also in Ginnheim is Döner, a bread filled with meat and salad - invented by turkish immigrants. So wherever in Germany you are - try a Döner!
